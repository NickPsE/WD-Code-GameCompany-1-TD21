About Capcom

From Osaka to the World.

Capcom Team

With beginnings as an arcade manufacturer in Japan during the early years of the video game industry, Capcom has built a reputation for creating cutting-edge technology and highly memorable video game franchises since its founding in 1983.

Over the course of its history, Capcom has created some of the most iconic characters and franchises in gaming. From the ubiquitous Street Fighter and Rockman franchises to the genre-defining Resident Evil and Devil May Cry series, Capcom has utilized its talent to create recognizable and entertaining games all over the world. (Capcom Singapore, 2024)

Mission and Vision

Mission

To revolutionize the gaming industry by creating the most interesting games in the world that and connect players through play.

Vision

To create an entertainment culture through our games, promoting creative content, and building connections in society.
